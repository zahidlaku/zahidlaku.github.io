Dec 2025 · AGU Fall Meeting, New Orleans

Presented my current work on Ostwald ripening in porous media

I presented a poster on curvature-driven evolution of trapped bubble populations in porous microstructures, combining microfluidic experiments with an image-based pore-network model. The work is motivated by applications in underground hydrogen storage, CO2 sequestration, and the design of efficient fuel cells and electrolyzers.
