About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Energy and Mineral Engineering at Penn State University. My research interests include porous media physics, pore-network modeling, and subsurface energy systems.

Research

  • Ostwald ripening of gas bubbles in porous microstructures
    Image-based pore network modeling to understand bubble evolution and its impact on fluid transport.
  • Multiphase flow in porous media
    Numerical simulation and analysis of two-phase flow and capillarity-driven processes.

Selected Projects

  • Pore Network Modeling Code – MATLAB implementation for simulating two-phase flow in complex pore networks.
  • Mineral Image Classification – Machine learning models for classifying mineral types from image data.
